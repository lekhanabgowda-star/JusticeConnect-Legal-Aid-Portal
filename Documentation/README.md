# JusticeConnect Legal Aid Portal

## Problem Statement
Many people in rural and low-income communities struggle to access legal help due to lack of awareness, high legal fees, and complex legal systems.

## Solution
JusticeConnect is a digital legal aid portal that connects individuals with verified lawyers, allowing them to submit cases, track case status, and schedule consultations online.

## Sustainable Development Goals (SDG)

### SDG 16 – Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions
The platform promotes equal access to justice for all by providing affordable legal services.



## Impact
This system simplifies the legal aid process and improves access to justice through technology.
